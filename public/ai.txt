LAW.AI - Indian Legal AI Platform for Advocates and Law Firms

LAW.AI is an AI-powered legal technology platform designed specifically for Indian lawyers, advocates, and law firms. It helps legal professionals work more efficiently by providing intelligent tools for their daily practice.

CORE FEATURES:

1. AI Legal Assistant
   - Ask legal questions in Hindi, English, or Hinglish
   - Get answers based on Indian law: IPC, CrPC, CPC, Evidence Act, NI Act, Constitution
   - Cite relevant sections and landmark judgments
   - Trained on Indian legal procedures and court practices

2. Case Tracker (CNR Tracking)
   - Track court cases using CNR (Case Number Record) numbers
   - Get real-time updates on case status
   - View next hearing dates and case history
   - Works with Supreme Court, High Courts, and District Courts

3. Legal Document Generator
   - Generate legal documents: Rental Agreements, NDAs, Legal Notices, Affidavits
   - Customized for Indian law and jurisdiction
   - Professional formatting ready for court submission

4. Judgment Summarizer
   - Upload court judgments and get AI-powered summaries
   - Extract key issues, arguments, and final orders
   - Identify relevant sections and precedents

5. Legal Research
   - Search Indian laws, acts, and case laws
   - Find relevant Supreme Court and High Court judgments
   - Get section-wise explanations with practical interpretations

6. Client CRM
   - Manage client information and appointments
   - Track case progress and deadlines
   - Set reminders for hearings and filings

7. Legal News
   - Stay updated with latest legal developments in India
   - News from LiveLaw, Bar & Bench, and official sources
   - Filter by practice area and court
